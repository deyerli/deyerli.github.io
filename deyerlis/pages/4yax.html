Natural Enemies" is a research project carried out by CABI-CREA and the Departmental Government of Santa Cruz, Bolivia, with the objective of establishing the foundations for agricultural self-sustainability through the natural balance provided by parasitic species in eradicating crop pests and reducing chemical applications.The 4YAX system is the data base. The 4YAX system provided the operational technical support for data collection in the field, species identification, and breeding
